Who can become a member

The club is open to any student, ranging from novice developers who are just starting, to advanced developer who want to further their skill

What should I carry when attending a workshop?

We recommend carrying a notebook, pen, and a laptop with you because more often than not we make our hand dirty with code. Most importantlu, carry along a healthy dose of curiosity and enthusiasm.
